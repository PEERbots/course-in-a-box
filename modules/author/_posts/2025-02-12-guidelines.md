## Before We Continue

Before we start with discussing how to author, make sure you've already gone through the basics of the control module. You need to understand how you or others would use the interactions you design, before you can effectively design them.

## Authoring Guidelines

In that vein, we recommend starting with some theory. Go through the [Peerbots Content Authoring Guidelines](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1OuuhuPrlIWlhrXEF_3tie8YI4bf3-kQNTYyFWuVF2l4/edit?usp=sharing).
